MOBILE BEHAVIOR NOTES

The site is built for phones first enough to work on Android and iPhone.

Current mobile behavior:
- Hero fills the phone screen.
- Title scales down with clamp CSS.
- Watch with Sound button becomes wide and easy to tap.
- Listen links stack vertically.
- YouTube video stays responsive in 16:9 ratio.
- Sections use reduced padding below 700px width.
- Motion reduction is supported for users who prefer reduced motion.

Important mobile video note:
Mobile browsers often block autoplay with sound.
The background video is muted, looped and playsinline to improve autoplay support.
Sound is enabled only after the user taps Watch with Sound.

Final test devices:
- Samsung Galaxy / Android Chrome
- iPhone Safari
- Desktop Chrome
