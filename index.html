The Story

Big Black Puppet

Big Black Puppet is an original alternative rock track by Sasha Persholja, built on raw groove, grit, and the signature Fejkavangard sound.

The music video features a fast-paced skateboarding journey through urban streets — a mix of night rides, daytime sessions, close-up action, and authentic street energy.

No actors. No filters. Just the street, the board, and the music.
